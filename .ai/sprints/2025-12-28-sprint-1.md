## Issues Encountered

### Issue 1: Composer Dependency Conflict
**Description:** Initial `composer require` failed due to PHP 8.4 incompatibility with `phpdocumentor/reflection` and locked `webmozart/assert` version.
**Solution:** Used `-W` flag (`--with-all-dependencies`) to allow downgrade of `webmozart/assert` from 2.0 to 1.12.1.

### Issue 2: PHPStan Deprecated Config Option
**Description:** PHPStan 2.x rejects `checkMissingIterableValueType` option (valid in 1.x).
**Solution:** Removed the deprecated option from `phpstan.neon`.

### Issue 3: Unmatched Ignore Patterns
**Description:** PHPStan failed because ignored error patterns had no matches in clean codebase.
**Solution:** Added `reportUnmatchedIgnoredErrors: false` to config.
